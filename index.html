To my favorite masterpiece,

They say art is supposed to make you feel something profound, to change the way you look at the world. By that definition, you are the greatest piece of art I’ve ever come across.

When I started putting this together, I wanted to give you something that lasts longer than a day. Something that reminds you—every single morning—how incredibly valued, loved, and appreciated you are. I really appreciate us being together even after so many years - despite all the ups and downs, and irrespective of all the fuck - ups. You make me want to be a better version of myself in every possible way.

When I first saw you, we were just 15-16yo kids, but in my mind I knew - this is how the woman of my dreams would be. I am so glad to have you, it cannot be expressed in words. Even after years, we are still progressing, working on ourselves to be better for each other, and having a supportive partner like you through it all makes me what I am today. Believe me, coming back to you every weekend is the only way I am able to get through my entire week in Mumbai. The urge that I want to give you a much much much better life keeps me working harder every single day. I just want to thank you for existing and for being mine.

Happy 26th Birthday, Bachuu. Here is a little digital museum dedicated entirely to you, your cosmic energy, and all the tiny things that make you, you.

Love always,
